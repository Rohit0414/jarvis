FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about our Odoo to BigQuery Connector.

Does it work with custom Odoo modules and fields?

Absolutely. The connector dynamically reads your entire Odoo database schema. This includes custom modules, custom fields added to standard models, and new custom models. You can select these for export just like standard Odoo fields.

How does the connector handle large volumes of data?

The connector is built for performance. It processes records in optimized batches to handle millions of rows efficiently. For very large initial syncs, we recommend running the export during off-peak hours. Subsequent incremental syncs will then be extremely fast, only processing new or modified records.

What are the ongoing costs?

You purchase the module license one-time from the Odoo App Store. There are no recurring fees from us. The only ongoing costs are for your Google BigQuery usage (storage and querying), which is billed directly by Google Cloud based on your consumption.

What happens if I delete a record in Odoo?

The connector is optimized for appending and updating data (upsert logic). It does not perform hard deletes in BigQuery to preserve historical data integrity. We recommend a data archival strategy within BigQuery, such as filtering out records based on an `active` flag or a `last_updated_date` field, which can be easily managed in your BI tool.

Where can I find documentation or get support?

Detailed documentation is available on this product page and on our official website at https://niyulabs.com. For technical support, bug reports, or any questions, please contact our dedicated support team at info@niyulabs.com. We're here to help!
