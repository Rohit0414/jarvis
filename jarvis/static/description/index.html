FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How does the AI understand my questions?

The module uses powerful Large Language Models (like GPT) to convert your natural language questions into precise Odoo queries. It understands context, dates, and relationships within your Odoo data.

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. The AI only receives your question and the non-sensitive schema of your database (table/field names) to construct a query. The query is then executed inside your own Odoo instance, and your actual business data never leaves your system.

Can I combine data from different modules?

Yes. This is a key feature. You can ask for "top sales reps by overdue invoice amount" and the AI will automatically create widgets by joining data from the Sales, and Accounting modules on a single dashboard.

What kind of visualizations can I create?

The AI automatically suggests the best chart type for your query, including bar charts, line graphs, pie charts, KPI cards, and detailed tables. You can then customize the visualization to your liking.

What are the costs involved?

You purchase the Niyu AI Dashboard Builder module from the Odoo App Store. The only ongoing cost is for your usage of the OpenAI API, which is billed directly by OpenAI based on the complexity and volume of your questions. There are no recurring subscription fees from Niyu Labs.
