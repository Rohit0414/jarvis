Common Questions

What do I need?

Odoo v14+, GCP Account with BigQuery API, and a Service Account.

Is it secure?

Yes. Data travels HTTPS directly to Google. We don't see or store your data.

Custom fields supported?

Yes! The schema reader detects custom fields and models automatically.

Are there monthly fees?

No. One-time module purchase. You only pay Google for BigQuery usage.

Can I schedule exports?

Yes. Use the built-in scheduler for hourly, daily, or weekly incremental syncs.
